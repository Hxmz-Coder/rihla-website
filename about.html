About Us

The Rihla Foundation is dedicated to making the sacred journey of Hajj accessible to Muslims who face financial hardship.

Our Story

Founded with the belief that no Muslim should be prevented from fulfilling the fifth pillar of Islam due to financial constraints, The Rihla Foundation was established to provide full sponsorships for Hajj.

By working with local mosques and community leaders, we identify individuals and families who are most in need, ensuring that our support reaches those who would otherwise never have the chance to embark on this sacred journey.

Our Values

Accessibility

We are committed to removing financial barriers so every Muslim can fulfill their duty of Hajj.

Community

We partner with mosques and local organizations to support those in need and foster unity.

Faith

Our mission is rooted in the belief that faith should never be limited by financial means.

Our Founders

We are a group of high school students from Seattle, Washington, who came together with one vision: to make the sacred journey of Hajj possible for underprivileged Muslims. What started as a simple idea among friends has grown into a nonprofit organization, rooted in our community and dedicated to ensuring that financial hardship never prevents a believer from fulfilling this pillar of faith.
